About me

Hi, I'm Dylan. (he/him) I'm a student from Metro Vancouver studying media and design. My projects involve but are not limited to...

  • Audio/Video: Final Cut Pro, Audacity
  • Graphics: Figma, Adobe CC, Inkscape
  • Music: Logic Pro, and a janky-ass setup...
  • Web: HTML + CSS, VSCodium
  • Writing: My brain, Obsidian (my brain)

I've been honing these crafts for about as long as I can remember. Most of it was just me "learning by doing." If you keep doing a thing, eventually you'll find your own way to do things!

This website is an archive of all of my projects/work, and my concentrated thoughts at the moment. It will be everything I do. It is me.

Current interest highlights: Metal Gear series (specifically MGS2), black midi (band), The Curse (2023-), literally anything media literacy-related...
