overview

This brand redesign was a complete overhaul of an existing company's (Browse AI) visual identity: logotype, typography, landing page and print layouts, branding applications, and social media posts.

This was a final project done solo for IAT 102: Graphic Design during my Spring 2024 term.

tools

  • hand sketches
  • Adobe: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign
  • Figma

objective

Browse AI is a company that creates web scraper bots that are "an easy, affordable, and reliable way to extract and monitor data from the web at scale."

Using their existing values and mission statements, I needed to completely rethink and develop a new brand identity for this company.

process

iteration

I sketched on paper to fully understand the brand: visual brain dump and mind map. The logotype went through different typefaces during ideation.

brand assets

logotype

The logotype is set in Source Sans 3, with a circle highlighting the AI portion of the text. The circle is an analogue to Browse AI's browse/scraping function.

typography

Source Sans 3 is set as the primary typeface. It's a humanist sans-serif that retains the "human" aspect.

Urbanist Bold is set as the heading typeface. It's a bold, geometric sans-serif which round, approachable features.

colour palette

In order to retain simplicity, there are three colours used. The main deep blue evokes stability, trust, and familiarity.

takeaways

Counter-intuitively, through exploring meanings and reasons behind a brand, I found that visual branding had surprisingly little to do with aesthetics as a means to an end. I found that understanding what, and how a brand operates will naturally inform the design through meaning.

In the future, I would focus even further on ideation and research in order to get fuller meaning into "why" I made a design.
